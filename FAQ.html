What does my membership include?

The membership includes access to our state-of-the-art gym which features top-of-the-line cardio and strength equipment, as well as group fitness classes that cater to all levels of fitness. In addition, the membership also includes the option for 24-hour access to the gym, so you can work out at any time that suits you

When do I need to cancel to avoid being charged for my next month's membership?

We ask that you cancel 30 days prior to your next billing date. This gives us ample time to ensure that your membership is cancelled so you aren't charged again.

What happens if I stop paying for my month-to-month membership?

Regardless of changing your credit card, a balance will see accrue on your account unless you request to cancel your membership. After a certain amount of time, your membership will go into collections

Can I use my spouse/child/friend's membership?

Under no circumstances can you share your PureFitness memberhsip. Doing so may result in the loss of your membership.

What are the certifications required to be a group fitness instructor?

Candidates should have relevant fitness or education certification, background or degree with a minimum of 2 years teaching experience. AFFA and/or ACE Certifications strongly recommended. CPR is also required.

What should I bring to the locker room?

Gym clothes, proper sneakers, a lock, workout towel. Plus, if you want to wash up after your workout, bring your toiletries, a bath towel and shower sandals (optional).

What is proper gym etiquette when using equipment?

Make sure to wipe down equipment after use. During peak times, please spend no more than 30 minutes on the cardio machines.
